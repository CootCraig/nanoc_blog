## Denon DL-103r

### Denon DL-103R Specifications

* Output: 0.25 mV
* Output impedance: 14ohms
* Stylus: 16.5 Micron round tip
* Coils: copper (6N)
* Frequency range: 20Hz-45kHz
* Tracking force: 2.3-2.7g
* Compliance 5x10-6cm/dyne
* Weight: 8.5g
