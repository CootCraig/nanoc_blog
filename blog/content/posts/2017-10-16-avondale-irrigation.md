<h3>Irrigation Timer Setup Sun May 13 2018</h3>

<pre>
sta. 1-3 delay is 7 min.
sta. 4-5 delay is 5 min.

sta. 1,2,3 cycle/soak is 3/1
sta. 4,5 cycle/soak is 2/1

sta. 1,2,3 on Prg A
station runtimes are 27 min. - 27/3 = 9 times through the 3 stations
Prg. A elapsed time is
  cycle=2; delay=7; sta=3; n=9; p (n * (sta * (delay + cycle)))
  => 243
  243 / 60 => 4; 243 % 60 => 3; 4h 3m;
  round up to 4h 15m

sta. 4,5 on Prg B
station runtimes are 28 min. - 28/2 = 14 times through the stations
Prg. B elapsed time is
  cycle=2; soak=5; sta=2; n=14; p (n * (sta * (soak + cycle)))
  => 196
  196 / 60 => 3; 196 % 60 => 16; 3h 16m
  round up to 3h 30m

start Prg A at 9:30pm
start Prg B at 2:00am
Prg B complete by 5:30am
</pre>

<h3>Irrigation Timer Setup Thu May 10 2018</h3>

<pre>
sta. 1-5 delay is 5 min.
sta. 1,2,3 cycle/soak is 3/1
sta. 4,5 cycle/soak is 2/1

sta. 1,2,3 on Prg A
sta. 1,2,3 to water each 3 minutes
3+5+3+5+3+5 = 24 min
24 min. watering per station is 8 cycles
8 * 24 = 192 min. = 3h 12m

sta. 4,5 on Prg B
sta. 4,5 to water each 2 minutes
2+5+2+5 = 14 min.
24 min. watering per station is 12 cycles
12 * 14 = 168 min. = 2h 48m

start Prg A at 10pm
start Prg B at 1:30am
Prg B complete by 4:30am
</pre>
