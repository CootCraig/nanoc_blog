<h2>

  Avocado Oil Vinaigrette
</h2>

<h4>Ingredients</h4>

<pre>
2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
4 teaspoons apple cider vinegar 
1/4 cup avocado oil 
Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper 
</pre>

<h4>Directions</h4>

In a small mixing bowl, whisk together the mustard and vinegar. Pouring
slowly, add a thin stream of avocado oil to the bowl, whisking with the
other hand. Continue to whisk until all of the oil has been poured into
the bowl and the ingredients have come together homogeneously. Season
with salt and pepper.

<h3>
  <a href="http://www.marthastewart.com/336150/walnut-vinaigrette" target="_blank">marthastewart.com/336150</a>
 Walnut Vinaigrette
</h3>

Alfred Portale makes this vinaigrette to dress his pear salad at Gotham Bar and Grill in New York City. 

<h4>Ingredients</h4>

<pre>
3 tablespoons red-wine vinegar
Star Wine Vinegar Red
2 teaspoons Dijon mustard
3/4 cup plus 2 tablespoons walnut oil
Coarse salt and freshly ground pepper
</pre>

<h4>Directions</h4>

In a medium bowl, whisk together vinegar and mustard. Gradually whisk
in the walnut oil until emulsified. Season with salt and pepper. Cover,
and refrigerate until ready to use. Before using, whisk well. Store in
an airtight container, refrigerated, up to 1 day.
