# design log

7'6 - 30" - 5"1/4

Pirsch rocker shows 3"3/4 thickness.  Bottom at nose at 5"3/4

(5.75 / 3.74) = (d1rocker / 5.25)

dirocker = (5.25 * 5.75) / 3.75 = 8.05 use 8"
